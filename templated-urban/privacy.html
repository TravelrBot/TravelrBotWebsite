Privacy

Please refer to our privacy statement below for any questions

Retainment of information

We do not ask for nor retain personal information of any kind. We only monitor usage for things like time spent using TravelrBot, number of visits, and other non-personal statistics that we can use to better serve you. No payment information will be requested or processed through this site. All such information will be processed directly with the ride service. If any such information is requested, please contact us at admin@travelrbot.com and refuse the request.

Sale of information

We do not retain any personal information and would not sell it even if we did. We take your privacy very seriously.
